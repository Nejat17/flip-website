Mission

We are a student-run organization focused on advocating for FLI (First-Gen and/or Low-Income) and that's cool ig. students and creating a community among us. Throughout the year, we host many different types of events and advocate for the FLI community in many ways.

History

FLIP was founded in 2010 to celebrate the end of the cold war
